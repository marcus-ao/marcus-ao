### About

Hi, I'm Marcus. I work in **data science and analytics** — asking good questions of data and turning the answers into decisions people can act on. My roots are in **Big Data Management & Application**, with a lasting interest in deep learning, NLP, and the engineering that turns models into something useful.

I also enjoy building clean, well-crafted software — this site among them. Away from the screen I read widely and write often, and *Diamonds and Pearls* is where those notes live.
